Giving

Cultivating a Culture of Generosity

We long to be a community of people living lives of holistic generosity in our time, talents and treasure; following the example set forth by Jesus who gave himself completely on our behalf.

Online

We accept online giving via PayPal.
Please designate if you are giving towards a specific commitment, need, or parish.

By Mail

You can mail a check, made out to Journey Church Tampa, to our central offices at 10022 Cristina Dr, Riverview FL 33569. Please designate if you are giving towards a specific commitment, need, or parish.

In Person

During a regular Sunday gathering there are envelopes in each chair and baskets that are conveniently placed in the parish for each person to place their gift.
