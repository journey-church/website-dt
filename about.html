Our Story

In the spring of 2012 the vision for a new congregation in Downtown Tampa was shared with the JCT Riverview Parish. After much prayer and many powerful moments of God’s favor toward this endeavor, Journey Church Tampa Downtown was planted in the spring of 2013 by a team of eleven people.

Members of this plant team continued to meet in the evenings, faithfully serving the city blocks around them where there were little signs of life in the wake of the recession that had swept through Tampa.

We as a church went through a transition in the early spring of 2015 bringing Pastor Corey Wolloff to the team as Parish Pastor and shifting our services to Sunday mornings. As the city has gained momentum and undergone incredible changes we have been right here in the middle of it all. We could not be happier with all that God is doing in Tampa.

In the Winter of 2018 we underwent a full and complete renovation of our meeting space in downtown creating a beautiful building in which to meet together and serve the city well. Our Kids Church has expanded rapidly, God has been faithful to our church and we are absolutely convinced that the best is yet to come.

The future is indeed bright. We welcome you to join in on the incredible story that God continues to write with our church in this city that we love.

The Gospel

By believing the Gospel, you are brought into a new community (1 Corinthians 12:13). In the church, gospel-changed Christians now slowly become freed from the inner slavery and the social exclusion that marked their lives before. The church is where Christians with transformed identities begin to live out (partially, but significantly) the changed economic, social, racial, and family relationships that will mark the future kingdom of God, when Christ comes to completely heal all the alienation in the world and renew the whole earth. The church becomes the 'sign of the kingdom' for a broken world.

Cultural Distinctives

Every organization has a culture/ethos. It’s a way of doing things. At JCT, we want to make sure that the culture of our church is very clear. In order to keep our culture from becoming watered down, we have clear cultural distinctive that we want to keep at the center of everything we do. You will see these working no matter the parish you attend. Our heart is to live out (become practitioners of) each of these.

Culture of Pursuit

We Long To Be A Community Of People That Daily Pursue An Intimate Relationship With Our Heavenly Father.

Culture Of Grace

We Desire To Be A Community Of People That Live By The Grace Of Jesus Christ, To Show Others The Same Grace That Jesus Has Shown Us, And To Teach Grace As The Only Way We Can Really Change.

Culture Of Renewal

We Long To Seek The Renewal Of Our Neighborhoods As We See God’s Kingdom Come In Tampa As It Is In Heaven.

Culture Of Family

We Aim To Be A Community Of People That Stand Beside One Another Through The Joys And Complexities That Make Up Our Daily Lives, Resolving Conflict, And Joining In Celebration With One Another As True Brothers And Sisters In Our Heavenly Father.

Culture Of Presence

We Desire To Be A Community Of People Marked By The Presence Of The Holy Spirit, Both In Our Daily Lives, And Our Corporate Gatherings.

Culture Of Generosity

We Long To Be A Community Of People Living Lives Of Holistic Generosity In Our Time, Talents And Treasure, Following The Example Set Forth By Jesus Who Gave Himself Completely On Our Behalf.

Covenant Membership

A Covenant is a promise, a deep commitment of joining in with something that is greater or more vast than any individual. By being in Covenant community with each other we push against the grain of cultural church consumerism and agree that we not only want to faithfully follow Jesus but that we want to faithfully follow HIm together. One for another, all committed to Jesus.
