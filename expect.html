Gospel Communities

Gospel Communities are what we call our small groups at Journey. Gospel communities meet throughout each neighborhood and provide the environment for building deep community with each other that a larger gathering may not allow. Each Gospel Community ranges in size from 5-20 people. In these groups, we eat together, laugh together, and practice the way and teachings of Jesus.

For more information on joining one of our current communities reach out to us at contact@jctdowntown.com

All Parish Gatherings

All Parish gatherings are times we set aside for all of our churches to get together and celebrate what God is doing as a whole in Journey Church Tampa. Each gathering is different. We gather for worship, picnics, special services, and fellowship. We celebrate each parish, and one another, as we rehearse the love that Jesus has for His people.
